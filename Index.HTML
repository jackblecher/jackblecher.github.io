Why Accounting?

I took accounting classes in high school which gave me my first glimpse into the field. Something that really attracted me was how incredibely versatile it is. Accounting is used in everything when it comes to business. As Warren Buffett said, "Accounting is the language of business." Accounting having high versatility allows me many different avenues when it comes to job opportunities in the future. Additionally, in my first classes I genuinely started to enjoy the accounting work. I've always had an analytical mind and accounting to me was enjoyable problem solving because there was always a right answer. I liked making all of the numbers balance out. Going forward, I am excited to start my career in accounting and plan on Gonzagas five year masters program for CPA certification. After this, I am open to many different options, but am currently looking towards the traditional route of joining one of the big four to get my career up and running.
